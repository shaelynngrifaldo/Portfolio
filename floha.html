Friends of the Lower Haw River Internship

During my internship with Friends of the Lower Haw River, I developed multiple outreach and educational products aimed at enhancing community engagement and environmental awareness. These projects focused on native plant and wildlife identification, combining field photography and creative design.

Wildflower Identification Guide

This field guide highlights native wildflowers along the Lower Haw River, combining species photography, identification tips, and habitat notes.

Flowering Shrubs and Trees Identification Guide

This guide introduces key shrub and tree species native to the Haw River ecosystem, emphasizing ecological functions and seasonal traits.

Snake Identification Guide

This educational pamphlet focuses on identifying local snake species and distinguishing between venomous and non-venomous varieties to promote coexistence and safety.
